About Joan & PUPPP’s

Joan has always shared a special bond with animals — one built on empathy, trust, and a deep belief in the emotional connection between humans and pets. As the founder of Posh Pet Boutique and SPAw, she spent years surrounded by furry characters, loving clients, and heartwarming stories.

Those stories — the funny ones, the messy ones, the meaningful ones — became the inspiration behind her book and the foundation for what’s next.

PUPPP’s stands for People Uniting with Purpose & Passion for Pets.

It’s a space to share knowledge, spark conversations, and inspire community-led action — without asking for donations. Just real people, doing what they can to make life better for animals everywhere.

Whether it’s helping shelters rethink their outreach, talking honestly about pet health, or advocating for change in labs and legislation — this project is about one thing: compassion in action.
